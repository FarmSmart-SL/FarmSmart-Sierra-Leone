Pest Control & Crop Diseases

Learn how to protect your crops from pests and diseases.

🐛 Fall Armyworm

Attack maize crops. Use early spraying and field monitoring.

🍃 Cassava Mosaic Disease

Causes yellow leaves. Remove infected plants immediately.

🌾 Rice Blast Disease

Affects rice leaves. Use resistant seeds and proper spacing.

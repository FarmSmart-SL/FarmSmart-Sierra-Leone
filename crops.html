Crop Advisory

Learn the best practices for growing major crops in Sierra Leone.

🌾 Rice

Plant in wet soil between May–July. Needs plenty water.

🌱 Cassava

Grows well in poor soil. Harvest after 9–12 months.

🌽 Maize

Best planted in rainy season with good spacing.

🥜 Groundnut

Needs sandy soil and moderate rainfall.

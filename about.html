About FarmSmart Sierra Leone

FarmSmart Sierra Leone is a digital agricultural platform designed to support farmers with reliable and easy-to-understand farming information.

🎯 Our Mission

To improve farming productivity through digital agricultural guidance.

🌍 Our Vision

To become the leading digital farming support system in Sierra Leone.

🤝 What We Do

We provide crop advice, pest control tips, and farming education.
